Inviting Submission: Isabella Beeton at
the Englishwoman's Domestic Magazine

Dissertation Digital Supplemental Materials

by Julie M. Sorge Way, 2023

This website houses the digital supplemental materials for my dissertation.

These include an interactive chronological index to open-access copies of the complete run of the EDM (1852-1879), a transcribed linguistic corpus of reader correspondence from the EDM between 1857-1861, interdisciplinary open educational resources on the EDM, and information on the project as a whole.
